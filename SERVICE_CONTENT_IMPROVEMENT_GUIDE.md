```markdown
## Financing & Payment Models

### Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)

- **No upfront cost** for client
- ACOB owns and operates the system
- Fixed price per kWh (₦40-₦70/kWh)
- 10-20 year contract terms
- **Best for:** Cash-flow constrained businesses

### Lease-to-Own

- Low upfront payment (10-20%)
- Monthly lease payments over 5-10 years
- Ownership transfer at end of term
- **Best for:** Businesses wanting eventual ownership

### Direct Purchase + Financing

- 20-30% down payment
- Bank financing for balance (7-12% interest)
- Immediate ownership and tax benefits
- **Best for:** Established businesses with credit access

### Performance-Based Financing

- Payment tied to actual energy generation
- Risk sharing between ACOB and client
- Flexible payment schedules
- **Best for:** Novel applications or credit-limited clients
```
